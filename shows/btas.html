Batman: The Animated Series

September 5, 1992 - January 16, 1999

This animated series conveys the dark mood of the original

"Batman" comic books. Unlike the light action "Batman"

show of the 1960s, Gotham City's Caped Crusader, Bruce

Wayne, is sometimes moody. And Robin's alter ego, Dick

Grayson, has a more-mature personality than in the original

series.
