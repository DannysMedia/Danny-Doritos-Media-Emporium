Oppenheimer

July 21, 2023

During World War II, Lt. Gen. Leslie Groves Jr. appoints

physicist J. Robert Oppenheimer to work on the top-secret

Manhattan Project. Oppenheimer and a team of scientists

spend years developing and designing the atomic bomb.

Their work comes to fruition on July 16, 1945, as they

witness the world's first nuclear explosion, forever changing

the course of history.
